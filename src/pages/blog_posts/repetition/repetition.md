## Repetition
In a talk from BYU-Idaho<sup> 1</sup>, Elder Bednar taught that repetition is a vehicle that allows the Spirit to teach. He used the multiple visitations of Moroni to Joseph Smith in one night as an example. Listed here are examples of repetition I've found in the scriptures during my studies.

- [1 Nephi 4:10-18](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/1-ne/4.10-18)

    The Spotify tells Nephi the times to kill Laban. Each time, Nephi gains a bit more understanding, and finally obeys.

- [3 Nephi 11:1-6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/11.1-6)

    Gathered around the temple, the Saints in the promised Land hear a voice but don't understand it. Each time, they try harder to hear and understand it until they understand God's message.

- [Acts 10:11-17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/acts/10.11-17)

    Peter has a vision in which God tells him to eat impure animals, but he refuses. This is repeated three times. After pondering and speaking with Cornelius, he realizes that God wants him to spread the gospel to everyone, Gentile and Jew alike.

- [Helaman 5:28-33](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/hel/5.28-33)

    A cloud of darkness covers the prisoners in the jail and speaks to them three times.

- [John 21:15-17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/john/21.15-17)

    Jesus asks Peter if he loves Him, and gives him the charge to preach.
